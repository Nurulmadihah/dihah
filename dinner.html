This summer special recipe can truly prove to be a game-changer of sorts, especially on days when you want something quick and impressive. It is easy to cook, and its texture is palatable to almost all meat-eaters.

Mango Chili Chicken

Total Cook Time: 35 mins Prep Time: 15 mins Cook Time: 20 mins Recipe Servings: 2

Ingredients of Mango Chili Chicken

500 gms Boneless chicken (cubed) | 1/2 cup Mango Pulp | 1/2 cup Bell Pepper (Cubed) | 1/2 cup Onion (Cubed) | 1/4 cup Zucchini (Chopped) | 1/2 cup Broccoli (Chopped) | 1/4 cup Carrot Chopped | 2 Green Chillies (Slit) | 1 cup All Purpose Flour | 1/2 cup Cornflour | 1 tbsp Ginger-Garlic Paste | 1/2 tbsp Chopped Garlic | 1 tsp Corn starch | 2 tbsp Soya Sauce | 1 tbsp Green Chilli Sauce | 1 tbsp Tomato ketchup

Step to Make Mango Chili Chicken

FIRST, The dish starts with coating the cubes of boneless chicken. Take a bowl, combine the all-purpose flour, corn flour, salt, black pepper, and ginger-garlic paste, and stir to combine for this recipe. Place the chicken cubes in the basin and generously coat them with the flour mixture. Set apart.

SECOND, Take a frying pan, add oil, and heat it in the meantime. Once finished, plop the chicken pieces into the oil and cook until crisp and golden. Please take note: Double frying the chicken pieces will result in more crispy chicken.

THIRD, Now for the sauce, take a separate pan, add 2 tbsp oil and let it heat. Add chopped green chillies and chopped garlic and fry for a few seconds.

FOURTH, Add onion (cubed) and cubed mixed bell peppers and fry on high heat for a minute.

FIFTH, Now is the moment to season the pan with salt and pepper to taste, as well as the soy sauce, sweet chilli sauce (if using), red chilli sauce, tomato ketchup, and vinegar.

SIXTH, Make a slurry out of cornstarch and 1/2 cup water, then pour it into the pan. Cook the cornstarch for one minute or until the sauce thickens. If necessary, add extra water.

SEVENTH, Add the fried chicken and mango pulp and give it a mix. Cook for 2 - 3 minutes.

SERVE AND ENJOY YOUR DINNER!
